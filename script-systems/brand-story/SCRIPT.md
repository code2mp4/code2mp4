# Brand Story — Script System
> Category: Brand & Identity
> Duration: 20-45s
> Scenes: 4-6
> Summary: Philosophy(big idea,slow,0-8s)→Problem(tension/gap,8-16s)→Solution(how we solve,16-26s)→Impact(aspirational vision,26-36s)→Identity(brand lockup+tagline,36-45s). Abstract concepts grounded in concrete metaphors. MAX 1 idea/scene, every scene quotable alone. Voice: philosophical,warm,human. No buzzwords(synergy,ecosystem,disruption). Arc: tension→release→resolution. Breathing room between every statement.

## Narrative Arc
Philosophy → Problem → Solution → Impact → Identity

## Information Density
- Abstract concepts allowed (this is brand, not product)
- Headlines: philosophical, memorable, 8-20 characters
- Body: 15-30 characters, poetic rhythm
- Maximum 1 idea per scene
- Every scene should be quotable on its own

## Pacing Map
| Section | Time | Duration | Feel |
|---------|------|----------|------|
| Philosophy | 0-8s | 8s | Slow, atmospheric — big statement |
| Problem | 8-16s | 8s | Tension builds — the gap or need |
| Solution | 16-26s | 10s | Release — how we solve it |
| Impact | 26-36s | 10s | Aspirational — the world after |
| Identity | 36-45s | 9s | Lockup — brand name + tagline |

## Voice & Tone
- Philosophical, warm, human
- Abstract ideas grounded in concrete metaphors
- "We" or brand name, not "I"
- Short sentences with breathing room
- One memorable phrase that anchors the story

## Hook Patterns
1. **Philosophical Statement** — "Less is more" — abstract truth first
2. **Map vs Compass** — Concrete metaphor for abstract idea
3. **Before/After** — Show the world before, then hint at after
4. **Single Word** — One word fills the screen, then expands

## Scene Templates
### 1. Philosophy (0-8s)
- ONE big idea statement
- Serif or humanist font (warmth)
- Slow reveal: scale 1.05→1 over 1.2s
- Generous padding, breathing room
- Background: solid color or subtle gradient

### 2. Problem (8-16s)
- The gap, tension, or need
- "In a complex world..." or equivalent
- Visual: abstract tension (contrast, negative space, dark)
- Text enters faster than Philosophy (0.5s vs 1.2s)

### 3. Solution (16-26s)
- How we address the problem
- 3 pillars/concepts presented sequentially
- Visual: clean, organized, resolving the tension
- Accent color emerges here

### 4. Impact (26-36s)
- Aspirational vision
- "Imagine..." or "A world where..."
- Visual: bright, open, hopeful
- Slow fade to this scene

### 5. Identity (36-45s)
- Brand name lockup
- Tagline
- One last philosophical echo
- Visual: centered, static, confident

## Anti-Slop Checklist
- [ ] Every scene is quotable alone
- [ ] Concrete metaphor, not abstract claim
- [ ] No buzzwords ("synergy", "ecosystem", "disruption")
- [ ] Arc has tension → release → resolution
- [ ] Brand name visible in first AND last scene
- [ ] Breathing room between every statement
- [ ] Not trying to explain everything — just the essence
